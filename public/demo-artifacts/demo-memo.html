Executive Summary

This SaaS Subscription Agreement with CloudBase Inc. presents High overall risk (score: 72/100). The two most serious issues are a minimal liability cap (1× monthly fees = $200) that leaves us with nearly no recourse for service failure, and unilateral modification rights that allow CloudBase to change pricing or features with 30 days' notice mid-term. The 90-day auto-renewal notice window and fully non-refundable fees compound the financial exposure. Legal counsel review is recommended before execution. We should not sign as-is without negotiating the liability cap and requesting an SLA addendum.
