2. SUBSCRIPTION TERM AND FEES

2.1 Initial Term. This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of twelve (12) months ("Initial Term").

2.2 Subscription Fees. Customer agrees to pay Provider a monthly subscription fee of $4,500 per month (the "Subscription Fee"), billed annually in advance. The total annual fee is $54,000, due within fifteen (15) days of the Effective Date.

2.4 Late Payment. Any payment not received within fifteen (15) days of the due date shall accrue interest at a rate of 2% per month (24% annually) compounding monthly, plus any collection costs.

2.5 No Refunds. All fees are non-refundable. Customer is not entitled to any refund or credit upon cancellation or termination of this Agreement, regardless of the circumstances.
